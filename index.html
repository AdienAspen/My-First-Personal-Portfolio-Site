I, The Coder

Hi! I’m Adien Aspen, an English language instructor and coder. I specialize in Python, Javascript, their libraries, and working with AI frameworks like Langchain, CrewAI, AI Forge, and Swarm.

My passion lies in blending tech with creativity, and I’m always exploring new ways to build and innovate.

When I’m not coding or teaching, I love sports, music, and the outdoors. Family gatherings keep me grounded. Every weekend, I hit the streets and parks of central Chile, where I live, to run a 10K—it’s my way of pushing myself.

Relevant Works

Revista/Inglidesk is a site dedicated to evaluate, review and score, based on expert assessments, the various mobile apps out there in the market, but with a special focus on only English apps for language acquisition. I entirely developed and put on public domain this very useful site in 2024. It relies on WordPress as the main CMS and works on very simple, effective, and nice theme. It also uses intensively style and UX/UI from "Elementor", the page builder.

For a complete review of English language mobile apps, visit Enlace al proyecto 1

Inglidesk is the mother site for English language learners to use it as a useful source of orientation. It provides tons of useful links, a blog with over 100 expert, professional, well-written posts, a central Q&A engine (forum-like), and many other amenities. It is supported on WordPress and uses a forum-blog WordPress theme called "Docy".

For high-quality ESL-English study material and orientation, visit Enlace al proyecto 2

EnglishClass Institute is a landing page for a micro-institute that provides online teaching and tutoring classes for ESL learners. It was entirely developed by me, and it relies on WordPress. It uses a host service by Hostinger, and it uses one of the various high-impact visual themes by "Elementor".

For high-quality ESL-English language teachers, visit Enlace al proyecto 3
